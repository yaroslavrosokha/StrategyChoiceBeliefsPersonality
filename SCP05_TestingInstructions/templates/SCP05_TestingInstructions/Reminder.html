  1. The experiment is made up of 25 matches.

  2. At the start of each match you will be randomly paired with another participant.

  3. You will select a plan that will make your choices for you in every round of the match. The other participant will also select a plan.

  4. The match will last a random number of rounds.

  5. When the match ends, you will again be randomly paired with another participant for the next match.

  6. At the end of the experiment, your total points will be converted into cash at the exchange rate of 200 points = $1. In addition, you will be paid your show-up fee of $5.
